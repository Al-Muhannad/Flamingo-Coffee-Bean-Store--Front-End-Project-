Arriyad Shumaisi

Arriyad Shumaisi

72.00 SAR

Description

Dried Cherbesa Sidamo from Ethiopia: It is grown and harvested in one of the ancient farms at an altitude of 1900 meters above sea level in the Sidamo region, where it has been processed by the dried method to produce a coffee with a medium texture, acidity and complex fruity. As the distinction of this coffee, Al-Shmaisi neighborhood is distinguished as one of the first neighborhoods that emerged after the demolition of the Riyadh wall.. Its street was an important artery for traffic, shopping and the beginnings of large restaurants The neighborhood consisted of a group of farms, and it was named al-Shmaisi in relation to the deacon who owned the largest areas of farms on the southern side.
